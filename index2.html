India's Engineering Legend
Sir M. Visvesvaraya, India's legendary engineer, invented automatic floodgates and built the Krishna Raja Sagara dam. He modernized irrigation, water supply, and hydroelectric projects, transforming agriculture and urban infrastructure, earning the Bharat Ratna and inspiring Engineers' Day in India.
